Add AnswrIT to Your Home Screen

This site works great as a standalone app. Pick your device below.

  1. Open AnswrIT in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen (the square with an arrow).
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add in the top-right corner.

AnswrIT will appear on your home screen and launch like a regular app.
